# Operations On-Call Handbook

Internal — Operations department.

## Incident severity
- SEV-1: customer-facing outage — page on-call within 5 minutes
- SEV-2: degraded service — acknowledge within 30 minutes
- SEV-3: minor issue — next business day

## Handoff
On-call rotates weekly. Document timeline, impact, and rollback in the postmortem template within 48 hours of SEV-1/2 resolution.
